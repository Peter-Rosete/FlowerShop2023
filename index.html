About Us.

Flower Shop based in Ventura County

We are a Family Owned business, that has been serving Ventura County for over 20 years. Located in the Santa Clara Cemetary. We make arrangements for all sorts of occasions; Anniversaries, Mother's Day, Funeral, Birthdays and more!

We have a huge assortment of flower arrangements, such as bouquets, vases, crosses, wreaths, and single flowers. We also have a variety of flowers, such as roses, daisies, baby's breath, sunflowers, carnations, and all in different colors!
